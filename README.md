# 📊 Sales Performance & Pricing Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents a **multi-page interactive Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance and pricing behavior across a business.

The dashboard goes beyond basic reporting to answer a key business question:

> **How do pricing decisions (discounts vs price uplift) impact overall revenue performance?**

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze sales pipeline performance  
- Evaluate pricing behavior (discounts vs uplift)  
- Identify top-performing:
  - Clients  
  - Sales agents  
  - Products  
- Provide actionable business insights  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

The solution consists of **three interactive dashboards**:

### 🧑‍💼 Client Analysis
- Revenue contribution by client segment  
- Pricing behavior across:
  - Company size  
  - Company age  
  - Workforce  
- Identification of high-value customers  

---

### 👥 Sales Team Performance
- Top-performing sales agents  
- Deal conversion (Won vs Lost)  
- Sales cycle analysis  
- Revenue contribution per agent  

---

### 📦 Product Performance
- Revenue by product  
- Product-level win rates  
- Discount and pricing behavior  
- Identification of high-performing products  

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

- **Total Revenue**
- **Target Revenue**
- **Win Rate**
- **Price Variance**
- **Price Uplift** (Revenue gained above standard price)
- **Price Discount** (Revenue lost due to discounts)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📉 Discounts exceeded price uplift  
  → Indicates potential revenue leakage  

- 📊 Enterprise clients contributed the most revenue  
  → Key segment for growth  

- 👥 Sales performance varies across agents  
  → Opportunity for optimization  

- 📦 Some products show strong pricing power  
  → Potential for strategic positioning  

---

  - Dimension Tables:
    - account 
    - Products  
    - Sales Team
    - engage date
